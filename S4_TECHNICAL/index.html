Filipino Menu

Restaurant Guide

Sisig

A sizzling Filipino dish made from chopped pork face and ears, mixed with chili and calamansi.

Kare-Kare

Kare-kare is a rich Filipino stew made with oxtail, vegetables, and a thick peanut sauce, traditionally served with bagoong for added flavor.

Halo-Halo

Halo-halo is a famous Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, beans, and leche flan, topped with evaporated milk and sometimes ube ice cream.

Sinigang

Sinigang is a traditional Filipino soup known for its sour and savory broth, typically made with tamarind, vegetables, and a choice of meat like pork, beef, shrimp, or fish.
